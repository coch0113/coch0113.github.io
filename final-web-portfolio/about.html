Hey there!
My name is Ash Cochran. Over the years I've have developed a love for photography and capturing images. I have always been drawn to the beauty of nature as well as human interactions. Where nature and the aesthetic of the outdoors is beautiful it never beats working with individuals to capture the moments that mean the most to the. This includes weddings, birthdays, even photos when they feel at peace or maybe a portrait for an important position. No matter the occasion, I love being able to capture it, allowing for my clients to have that memory with them always.
